About meww.ing

meww.ing is a comprehensive, evidence-based resource on mewing and proper tongue posture. We aim to cut through the noise and provide clear, actionable guidance backed by scientific research on nasal breathing, myofunctional therapy, and orthotropics.

Our Approach

Every guide and article on meww.ing references peer-reviewed research where available. We link directly to PubMed studies on nasal breathing and myofunctional therapy, and we clearly distinguish between established science and anecdotal evidence. We don't make exaggerated claims — we tell you what the research shows and what remains unproven.

Editorial Standards

Our content is written and reviewed by researchers familiar with orthotropic principles and myofunctional therapy. We regularly update articles as new research becomes available. If we cite a claim, we provide a source. If something is speculative, we say so.

Why We Built This

Mewing information online is scattered across Reddit threads, YouTube videos, and forums — much of it contradictory or exaggerated. We created meww.ing to be the single, reliable reference that answers every mewing question with clarity and honesty.

Disclaimer

meww.ing is for educational purposes only. It is not medical advice. If you have orthodontic concerns, TMJ issues, or breathing problems, consult a qualified healthcare professional.
